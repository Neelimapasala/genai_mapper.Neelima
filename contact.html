FAQ

Quick answers
Is GenAI Mapper free?
Yes — fully free for academic and research use. All features including entity extraction, parse trees, citation tracking, and AI summaries are available at no cost.
What file formats are supported?
PDF, DOCX, TXT, and CSV files up to 50 MB. You can also paste raw text directly into any analysis tool.
Is my data kept private?
All uploaded files are processed in memory and deleted immediately after analysis. Nothing is stored on our servers — your research stays private.
Which AI model is used?
Groq LLaMA-3.3-70B powers summaries, parse trees, and all chatbots. spaCy (en_core_web_sm) handles real-time NER and POS tagging.
Can I collaborate or contribute?
Absolutely — research collaborations and academic partnerships are very welcome. Use the form on this page and select "Research collaboration" as your topic.
